Privacy Policy for Expentia

Effective Date: October 22, 2025
App: Expentia - AI-Powered Expense Management
Developer: Expentia Team

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Expentia ("we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile application. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency about our data practices.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Account Information

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Email address
  • Username
  • Password (encrypted)

2.2 Expense Data

To provide expense tracking services, we collect:

  • Transaction amounts and descriptions
  • Categories and payment methods
  • Dates and times of expenses
  • Budget information

2.3 Receipt Images

When you use our receipt scanning feature:

  • Images of receipts captured through your device camera
  • Extracted text data from receipts using OCR technology
  • Receipt metadata (date, merchant, amount)

Camera Permission Purpose: We use your device's camera exclusively for scanning receipts to automatically extract expense information. This feature helps you quickly add expenses by taking photos of receipts instead of manual entry.

What We Do:

  • Capture receipt images only when you actively use the scan feature
  • Process images locally on your device when possible
  • Extract text data (merchant, amount, date) from receipt images

What We Don't Do:

  • Access your camera without your explicit action
  • Take photos automatically or in the background

4. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Provide expense tracking and budget management services
  • Process and categorize your expenses automatically
  • Generate spending reports and analytics
  • Sync your data across your devices
  • Improve our app features and user experience
  • Provide customer support

5. Data Storage and Security

5.1 Data Storage

Your data is stored securely using:

  • Encrypted databases with industry-standard security
  • Secure cloud infrastructure
  • Regular security updates and monitoring

5.2 Data Security

We implement multiple security measures:

  • End-to-end encryption for sensitive data
  • Secure user authentication
  • Regular security audits
  • Limited access to personal data by our team

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties, except:

7. Subscription and Payment Information

For premium subscriptions:

  • We do not store your payment card information
  • We receive only subscription status and basic transaction data
  • Subscription management follows platform policies
